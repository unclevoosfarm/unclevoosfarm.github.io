Farms in Sandakan: Why Visitors Choose Uncle Voo's Farm

If you are searching for farms in Sandakan, Uncle Voo's Farm is one of the most family-friendly countryside destinations in Sandakan, Sabah. Located on Jln Wing Sang, this working farm combines comfortable homestay accommodation, fresh local produce, and guided farm experiences. Visitors can enjoy a calm rural environment while staying close to the city.

Uncle Voo's Farm address: Jln Wing Sang, 90000 Sandakan, Sabah, Malaysia
Contact: +60 16-805 1021

What you can do at this Sandakan farm

  • Book a private farm stay for families and small groups.
  • Join a guided farm tour with animal interaction and nature walks.
  • Buy farm products such as duck eggs, kampung chicken eggs, and seasonal fruits.
  • Take photos in a tropical countryside setting in Sandakan.

Farm stay and homestay near Sandakan town

Many travelers look for a farm stay in Sandakan because they want a slower pace, fresh air, and a memorable local experience. Uncle Voo's Farm offers a homestay setup with practical amenities, making it suitable for multi-generational family trips, weekend reunions, and nature-focused holidays.

Fresh produce from a real working farm

Besides accommodation, Uncle Voo's Farm is known for its produce and farm goods. Depending on season and availability, visitors can inquire about eggs, ducks, goats, vegetables, and local fruits. This direct farm-to-customer model gives guests a more authentic connection to agriculture in Sabah.

Planning your visit

For bookings, farm tour schedules, and product availability, contact Uncle Voo's Farm directly. Advance booking is recommended for weekends and holidays in Sandakan.
